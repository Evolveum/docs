This book is all about midPoint. MidPoint is an open source identity management and governance system. MidPoint is comprehensive and quite complex system and it takes some time and skill to master it. This book is a quide through the wonderland of identity management and governance with midPoint.

This book is written by egineers for engineers. You will not find sales material or business presentations in this book. It is writen by people that develop, maintain and support midPoint. The book starts with an introduction to Idenatity and Access Management (IAM) concepts, provides overview of midPoint and then it dives down to technical topics.
